How much does roof repair cost in Toronto?

Roof repair costs in Toronto typically range from $300 to $1,500 depending on the damage extent, material type, and roof accessibility. Emergency repairs may cost more. We provide free estimates.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements in the GTA take 1-3 days. Larger homes, complex designs, or commercial buildings may take longer. Weather conditions can also affect the timeline.

Do you offer emergency roofing services?

Yes, Blue Knight Roofing provides 24/7 emergency roofing services across Toronto and the GTA. We respond quickly to storm damage, leaks, and urgent repairs.

What roofing materials do you work with?

We install and repair asphalt shingles, metal roofing, TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, flat roof membranes, and more. We help you choose the best material for your property and budget.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Blue Knight Roofing is fully licensed, insured, and WSIB-covered. We comply with all Ontario building codes and manufacturer installation requirements.

Do you serve areas outside Toronto?

Absolutely. We serve the entire Greater Toronto Area including Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Oakville, Burlington, Newmarket, Aurora, and surrounding communities.
