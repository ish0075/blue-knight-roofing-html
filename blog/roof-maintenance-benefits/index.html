A lot of roofing material manufacturers (especially for membranes and commercial systems) require proof of maintenance. If the roof fails and there's no record of inspections or upkeep, the warranty can be denied.

Having Blue Knight Roofing Contractors document maintenance for your property in York, North York, King, King City, Stouffville, Aurora, Newmarket, East Gwillimbury, Georgina, Vaughan, Caledon, or Orillia gives you a paper trail if you ever need to make a warranty claim.
