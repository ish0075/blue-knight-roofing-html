Frequently Asked Questions About Full Roof Replacement

How long does a residential roof replacement take?

Most residential roofs are completed in 1-3 days depending on size, pitch, and complexity. We provide a detailed timeline before work begins.

Do you remove and dispose of the old roof?

Yes. Complete tear-off and disposal is included. We also inspect the roof deck underneath and repair any damaged boards before installing new roofing.

What shingle brands do you install?

We install GAF Timberline, CertainTeed Landmark, IKO Cambridge, and other premium architectural shingles. We help you choose the best option for your home and budget.

How much does a full roof replacement cost?

Costs depend on roof size, pitch, material choice, and complexity. Average GTA homes range from $8,000-$18,000. We provide free detailed estimates.
