Ice Dam Prevention & Removal

Ice dams form when heat escapes through your roof, melting snow that refreezes at the eaves. This backs up water under your shingles, causing leaks, mold, and structural damage. We address ice dams at the root cause — not just the symptoms.

Key Features of Our Ice Dam Solutions

  • Root Cause Analysis: We identify why ice dams form on YOUR roof — poor insulation, blocked vents, or both.
  • Insulation Upgrades: Attic insulation improvements to stop heat from escaping through your roof deck.
  • Ventilation Fixes: Proper soffit, ridge, and box vent installation to keep your attic cold and ice-free.
  • Heat Cable Installation: Strategic heat cable placement along eaves and in valleys to prevent ice buildup.

Frequently Asked Questions About Ice Dam Solutions

What causes ice dams?

Ice dams form when heat escapes from your living space through the attic, warming the roof deck and melting snow. The meltwater flows to the cold eaves where it refreezes, creating a dam that backs water up under your shingles.

Are heat cables a permanent solution?

Heat cables manage symptoms but don't fix the root cause. The best permanent solution combines proper attic insulation and ventilation. We often recommend cables as a supplementary measure alongside insulation upgrades.

Can ice dams cause serious damage?

Yes. Ice dams can cause interior leaks, ceiling and wall damage, mold growth, rotted roof sheathing, damaged gutters, and dangerous falling ice. Addressing them promptly prevents expensive repairs.

When should I call about ice dams?

Call immediately if you see large icicles, water stains on ceilings during winter, or ice buildup along your eaves. For prevention, schedule insulation and ventilation assessment before winter.
