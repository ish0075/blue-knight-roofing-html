Frequently Asked Questions About Shingle Roof Repairs

How much does shingle repair cost?

Simple repairs for a few shingles typically cost $200-$500. Larger areas of damage cost more. We provide free estimates before any work begins.

Can you match my existing shingles?

In most cases, yes. We carry a wide range of shingle brands and colors. For older or discontinued shingles, we find the closest match available.

How long do shingle repairs last?

Quality shingle repairs last the remaining lifespan of your roof. We use proper installation techniques and materials to ensure a permanent fix.

Should I repair or replace my shingle roof?

If damage is limited to a small area and the rest of your roof is in good condition, repair makes sense. If your roof is 20+ years old with widespread issues, replacement is more cost-effective long-term.
