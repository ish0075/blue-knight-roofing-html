Industrial Roofing Services

Industrial facilities demand roofing systems that can handle heavy equipment loads, chemical exposure, extreme temperatures, and continuous operation requirements. Our industrial roofing team delivers engineered solutions for the toughest environments.

  • Large-Scale Installations: Engineered roofing systems for warehouses, factories, and distribution facilities of any size.

  • Repairs & Retrofits: Repairs, re-roofing, and system upgrades for aging industrial roofs with minimal production downtime.

Frequently Asked Questions About Industrial Roofing Services

What types of industrial facilities do you service?

We service warehouses, manufacturing plants, distribution centres, food processing facilities, cold storage, automotive shops, and all other industrial buildings throughout the GTA.

Can you work around our production schedule?

Yes. We offer evening, night, and weekend crews to minimize impact on your operations. We develop phased work plans that keep your facility running.

What roofing systems do you use for industrial buildings?

Depending on your requirements, we install TPO, EPDM, PVC, modified bitumen, standing seam metal, and built-up roofing systems. We engineer the solution based on load requirements, chemical exposure, and energy goals.

Do you handle roofs with heavy equipment on them?

Yes. We work around and accommodate HVAC units, exhaust systems, solar panels, and other rooftop equipment. We install proper equipment pads and supports as part of the roofing system.
