Frequently Asked Questions About Emergency Roofing Services

What qualifies as a roofing emergency?

Roofing emergencies include: active leaks causing interior water damage, storm damage with exposed roof deck, fallen trees or debris on your roof, complete or partial roof collapse, and any situation where your property is at immediate risk of further damage.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

Our emergency team is available 24/7 and we strive to arrive within 1-2 hours of your call depending on location and current demand. We prioritize based on severity to address the most urgent situations first.

Do you provide temporary repairs or permanent fixes?

We first perform emergency mitigation (tarping, board-up, leak stop) to protect your property immediately. We then assess the damage and provide options for permanent repairs, which can often begin within days.

Will you work with my insurance company?

Yes, we work with all major insurance companies and can help document damage, provide detailed estimates, and communicate with adjusters to support your claim.

What should I do while waiting for emergency service?

If safe, try to contain interior water damage with buckets and towels. Move valuables away from leak areas. Do NOT go on your roof. Take photos of damage if possible. Our team will handle everything else when we arrive.
