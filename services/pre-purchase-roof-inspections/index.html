Frequently Asked Questions About Pre-Purchase Roof Inspections

Why do I need a separate roof inspection when buying a home?

General home inspectors provide a basic roof overview but lack roofing expertise. Our specialist inspection identifies issues a general inspector may miss and provides accurate repair cost estimates.

Can my realtor schedule the inspection?

Absolutely. We regularly work with realtors and can coordinate directly for access and scheduling. We understand the time-sensitive nature of real estate transactions.

What do you look for during a pre-purchase inspection?

We assess shingle/material condition, flashing, vents, chimneys, gutters, soffit, fascia, attic ventilation, insulation, signs of past leaks, and overall structural integrity.

How does the inspection help with negotiations?

Our detailed report with repair cost estimates gives buyers concrete evidence to negotiate the purchase price or request seller repairs before closing.
