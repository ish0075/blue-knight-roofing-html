Frequently Asked Questions About Flat Roof Repairs

What causes flat roof leaks?

Common causes include ponding water, membrane punctures, seam separations, flashing failures, and deterioration from UV exposure. Our inspection identifies the root cause before any repair.

How long does a flat roof repair take?

Most flat roof repairs are completed within a single day. More extensive damage involving structural work may take 2-3 days.

Can you repair all flat roof types?

Yes. Our technicians are trained in TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, built-up roofing (BUR), and PVC membrane systems.

Is it worth repairing or should I replace my flat roof?

If damage is localized and the roof is under 15 years old, repair is usually the best option. We provide honest assessments comparing repair vs. replacement costs.
