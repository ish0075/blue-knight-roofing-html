Frequently Asked Questions About Skylight Repairs

Why is my skylight leaking?

The most common causes are deteriorated flashing, failed seals, cracked glass, or improper original installation. Ice dam backup near skylights is also common in Ontario winters.

Should I repair or replace my skylight?

If the frame and glass are in good condition, replacing the flashing and seals is cost-effective. If the unit is over 20 years old, foggy, or has frame damage, replacement is usually the better investment.

Can you repair any skylight brand?

Yes. We service VELUX, Fakro, Sun-Tek, and all other brands. As VELUX certified installers, we have access to OEM parts and flashing kits.

How much does skylight repair cost?

Basic flashing and seal repairs typically cost $400-$800. Full skylight replacement ranges from $1,500-$3,500 including installation. We provide free estimates.
