Frequently Asked Questions About Fire & Water Damage Restoration

How quickly can you respond to fire or water damage emergencies?

We provide 24/7 emergency response services and can typically arrive on-site within 1-2 hours of your call. Our rapid response team is equipped with professional water extraction equipment and restoration tools.

What areas do you serve for damage restoration?

We serve Toronto, North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Markham, and surrounding GTA areas.

Will my insurance cover storm and fire damage restoration?

Most homeowner and commercial property insurance policies cover storm, fire, and water damage restoration. We work directly with insurance companies and help with all documentation.

What is included in your water damage restoration process?

Our process includes emergency water extraction, moisture detection, structural drying, dehumidification, antimicrobial treatment, content cleaning, and complete property restoration.

How long does fire damage restoration take?

Timelines vary based on damage extent. Minor smoke damage may take 2-3 days, while major fire restoration can take several weeks. We provide detailed timelines after assessment.

Do you handle both residential and commercial damage?

Yes, we provide professional restoration services for residential properties (homes, condos) and commercial properties (offices, retail, warehouses, industrial facilities).
