Frequently Asked Questions About Commercial Roofing

What commercial roofing systems do you install?

We install all major commercial roofing systems including TPO (Thermoplastic Olefin), EPDM (rubber membrane), modified bitumen, built-up roofing (BUR), and metal roofing. We'll recommend the best system for your building and budget.

How do you minimize business disruption during roofing work?

We work closely with you to schedule work during off-hours when possible, section off work areas, and maintain a clean, safe job site. Our experienced crews work efficiently to complete projects on schedule.

Do you offer commercial roof maintenance programs?

Yes, we offer customized preventive maintenance programs that include regular inspections, debris removal, drain clearing, and minor repairs. This extends your roof's lifespan and prevents costly emergency repairs.

How long do commercial roofs last?

Commercial roof lifespan varies by material: TPO and EPDM typically last 20-30 years, modified bitumen 15-20 years, and metal roofing 40-60 years. Proper maintenance significantly extends these lifespans.

Can you work on occupied buildings?

Absolutely. We regularly perform roofing work on occupied retail, office, and industrial buildings. We coordinate closely with property managers to ensure minimal disruption to tenants and customers.
