Frequently Asked Questions About Flat Roof Repairs (Residential)

Why does my garage flat roof keep leaking?

Common causes include ponding water, membrane deterioration, failed flashing where the flat roof meets the house wall, and blocked drains. We identify and fix the root cause.

What flat roof system is best for residential use?

EPDM rubber and modified bitumen are popular for residential flat roofs. TPO is also excellent for energy efficiency. We recommend the best option based on your specific situation.

Can you convert my flat roof to a sloped roof?

Yes, we can install a tapered system or build a slope over your existing flat roof. This is common for garages with persistent ponding water issues.

How long do residential flat roofs last?

With proper installation and maintenance, residential flat roofs last 15-25 years depending on the membrane type. Regular inspections extend this significantly.
