Frequently Asked Questions About Flat Roof Replacement & Reroofing

How long does a flat roof replacement take?

Timelines depend on building size. Small commercial roofs (under 5,000 sq ft) typically take 3-5 days. Larger projects may take 1-3 weeks. We can phase work to minimize business disruption.

Which flat roof system is best?

It depends on your building, budget, and priorities. TPO offers excellent energy efficiency, EPDM is durable and cost-effective, and modified bitumen provides multi-layer protection. We recommend the best fit after inspection.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes. We handle all municipal permits, building code compliance, and arrange required inspections for your flat roof replacement project.

Can you work on occupied commercial buildings?

Absolutely. We schedule work to minimize noise and disruption, section off work areas, and maintain a clean, safe job site for tenants and customers.
