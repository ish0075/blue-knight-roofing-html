Frequently Asked Questions About Residential Roofing

What residential roofing services do you offer?

We offer shingle repairs, flat roof repairs, leak detection, pre-purchase inspections, full roof replacements, ice dam solutions, attic ventilation improvements, and skylight repairs for homes across the GTA.

How do I know if I need a roof repair or replacement?

If your roof is under 20 years old with localized damage, repair is usually sufficient. If it's older with widespread issues, sagging, or multiple leaks, replacement may be more cost-effective. We provide honest assessments.

Do you work with realtors for pre-purchase inspections?

Yes. We regularly partner with realtors and home inspectors to provide detailed roof condition reports for buyers. Our reports include estimated remaining lifespan, needed repairs, and approximate costs.

How much does a residential roof replacement cost?

Costs vary based on roof size, pitch, material choice, and complexity. We provide free detailed estimates. Contact us for an accurate quote for your specific home.

Can you fix a flat roof on my garage?

Absolutely. We specialize in flat roof repairs and replacements for garages, additions, and modern home designs using TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen systems.
