Frequently Asked Questions About Roof Repair

How quickly can you repair my roof?

We offer same-day emergency repairs for urgent issues like active leaks. For non-emergency repairs, we typically schedule within 1-3 business days depending on our current workload.

How do I know if I need roof repair or replacement?

Several factors determine this: roof age, extent of damage, and cost comparison. Generally, if repairs would cost more than 30% of a new roof, or if your roof is over 20 years old with multiple issues, replacement may be more cost-effective. We provide honest assessments.

Do you repair all types of roofs?

Yes, we repair asphalt shingle roofs, flat roofs (TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen), metal roofs, and more. Our technicians are trained in all major roofing systems.

Will my insurance cover roof repairs?

Most homeowner insurance policies cover storm damage and sudden accidental damage. We can help document damage and work with your insurance company on claims.

What are signs I need roof repair?

Common signs include: visible leaks or water stains, missing or damaged shingles, granules in gutters, sagging areas, daylight visible through roof boards, and high energy bills. Schedule an inspection if you notice any of these.
