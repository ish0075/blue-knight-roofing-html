Preventative Maintenance Programs

Extend your commercial roof's lifespan by years with our customized preventative maintenance programs. Regular inspections and proactive repairs prevent costly emergencies and help you budget predictably for roof care.

Key Features of Our Preventative Maintenance Programs

  • Scheduled Inspections: Semi-annual or quarterly inspections tailored to your roof type and building needs.
  • Preventive Repairs: Minor repairs addressed immediately during inspections before they become major issues.
  • Detailed Reporting: Comprehensive condition reports with photos, findings, and maintenance recommendations.
  • Budget Planning: Predictable annual costs and long-term capital planning for roof replacement.

Benefits of Professional Preventative Maintenance Programs

  • Extend Roof Life: Add 5-10 years to your roof's lifespan
  • Reduce Costs: Prevent expensive emergency repairs
  • Documentation: Full history for insurance and compliance
  • Priority Service: Members get priority scheduling

Frequently Asked Questions About Preventative Maintenance Programs

What does a maintenance visit include?

Each visit includes a full roof inspection, debris removal, drain and gutter clearing, caulking touch-ups, minor repairs, and a written condition report with photos.

How often should commercial roofs be maintained?

We recommend semi-annual visits (spring and fall) for most commercial roofs. High-traffic roofs or those with heavy equipment may benefit from quarterly visits.

How much can maintenance save me?

Preventative maintenance typically costs 1-2% of a roof's replacement value annually but can prevent up to 70% of common roof failures, saving tens of thousands in emergency repairs and premature replacement.

Can you maintain roofs installed by other companies?

Yes. We maintain all flat and commercial roof types regardless of who installed them. We'll assess your current roof condition and develop a customized maintenance plan.
