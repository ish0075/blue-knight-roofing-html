Residential Roof Leak Detection

A roof leak left undetected can cause thousands in damage — from ruined insulation to mold growth and structural rot. Our technicians systematically trace leaks through your attic, roofing layers, and flashing to find the exact entry point and stop it for good.

Key Features of Our Roof Leak Detection

  • Systematic Tracing: We trace leaks from interior stains back through the attic and roof layers to the exact entry point.
  • Attic Inspection: Thorough attic assessment for moisture, mold, rot, and ventilation issues contributing to leaks.
  • Water Testing: Controlled water testing to confirm leak locations before cutting into anything.
  • Immediate Repair: Once found, we repair the leak source on the same visit whenever possible.

Frequently Asked Questions About Roof Leak Detection

I see a water stain on my ceiling — is it a roof leak?

Possibly. Ceiling stains can result from roof leaks, plumbing issues, or condensation. Our inspection determines the exact source and whether it's a roofing issue.

Can a leak appear far from its source?

Yes. Water often travels along rafters, sheathing, or insulation before dripping down, making the stain appear far from the actual roof penetration. That's why systematic tracing is essential.

How much does leak detection cost?

Our inspection fee is credited toward any repairs. For most homes, detection and repair can be completed in a single visit.

What if the leak only happens during certain conditions?

Some leaks only appear during wind-driven rain, ice dam conditions, or heavy snowmelt. We assess weather patterns and roof conditions to identify intermittent leak sources.
