Frequently Asked Questions About Roof Leak Detection

How does infrared leak detection work?

Infrared cameras detect temperature differences on the roof surface. Wet insulation retains heat differently than dry areas, allowing us to map moisture intrusion without any destructive testing.

Is electronic leak detection destructive?

No. Electronic field vector mapping is completely non-destructive. A low-voltage current is applied to the membrane surface, and breaches are detected by measuring electrical conductivity changes.

How accurate is leak detection?

Our advanced methods can pinpoint leak sources to within inches, far more accurate than visual inspection alone. This saves money by targeting repairs precisely.

When should I get leak detection done?

Whenever you notice interior water stains, unexplained moisture, or increasing energy bills. Also recommended before purchasing a property and as part of regular maintenance.
